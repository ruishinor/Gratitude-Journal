About this Practice

Gratitude turns what we have into enough.

This site is a digital refuge designed to disrupt stress and provide a moment of positive reflection. Taking a moment to answer just one of these questions can shift your mindset from what is lacking to what is present.

The Science

Research shows that focusing on positive elements of your life significantly enhances happiness and health by shifting attention away from negative bias. This collection of 90 prompts is pulled directly from The Complete Gratitude Workbook.
