About this website

This website is a digital space designed to reduce stress and give you a moment for positive reflection. By taking the time to answer just one of these questions, you can shift your mindset from what you don't have to what you do have. You don't have to answer them on the site itself, but if you do submit it, it might help others get past a difficult moment in the future.

The Science

Research shows that focusing on the good things in our lives can significantly improve our happiness and health. Practicing gratitude reduces stress and improves our mood by shifting our attention away from problems and towards the positive.
